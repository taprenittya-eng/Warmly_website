White Sauce Pasta
🕐 30 min total 🔥 Medium difficulty ⭐ 4.6 (137 reviews) 👤 2 servings
Lunch/DinnerFancy foodCreamyCheesyVeggies
Creamy, cheesy, and irresistibly delicious, White Sauce Pasta is a perfect blend of rich flavors and comforting goodness. Every bite is smooth, indulgent, and satisfying.

How to make it

1

Boil two cups of pasta with a pinch of salt and a little oil. Once cooked, strain, wash with cold water and toss with a little oil to prevent sticking.

2

Meanwhile, sauté all veggies (corn, capsicum, onion, mushrooms, broccoli) in 1 tsp of oil until lightly cooked. Keep aside.

3

In a wok, add 2 tbsp butter and 2 tbsp all-purpose flour. Mix until golden brown, then add 2 cups of milk and some pasta water. Keep stirring continuously to avoid lumps. Adjust consistency with water or milk.

4

Add chilli flakes, oregano, lots of cheese, all the cooked veggies, and a pinch of salt to the sauce. Stir well.

5

Add the boiled pasta to the sauce and mix properly. Serve hot garnished with lots of shredded cheese. 🍽️

Ingredients — 2 servings

Pasta2 cups
Butter2 tbsp
All purpose flour2 tbsp
Mixed veggies1 of each
Milk2 cups
Chilli flakes1 tsp
Oregano1 tsp
Cheese, gratedas needed
Oil3 tbsp
Salt & pepperto taste

💡 Chef's tip (by Nittya): Add more pasta water to the sauce to make it extra saucy. Be careful with salt as it is added at multiple stages — a little each time is the key!

Dal Khichdi
🕐 25 min total 🔥 Easy difficulty ⭐ 4.8 (142 reviews) 👤 2 servings
DinnerComfort foodSlow cookHealthyLight
Wholesome, flavorful, and comforting, Dal Khichdi is a timeless classic loved for its simplicity and goodness. It's the perfect meal for any time of the day.

How to make it

1

In a pressure cooker, cook Dal and Rice together with a pinch of salt and turmeric for 4-5 whistles.

2

Take a wok and prepare the Tadka — add oil, cumin seeds, kadi patta, green chillies, red chillies, ginger garlic paste and finely chopped tomatoes.

3

After tomatoes soften, add salt to taste, hing, chilly powder, coriander powder, turmeric, and garam masala. Let it all cook on low flame.

4

When the masala is cooked, add the dal and rice mixture to the wok and stir so the masala is evenly mixed.

5

Add boiling hot water to the khichdi as per your preferred consistency. Mix properly and cook for 5-6 more minutes. Serve with Papad or Achaar. 🍽️

Ingredients — 2 servings

Rice2.5 cups
Dal3 cups
Tomato1 large
Garlic cloves, minced4
Fresh ginger, grated1 tbsp
Ground cumin2 tsp
Ground coriander1 tsp
Turmeric powder½ tsp
Oil2 tbsp
Salt & pepperto taste

💡 Chef's tip (by Nittya): Add a tablespoon of butter in the oil before making the Tadka, and also while serving the Khichdi. It makes all the difference!
